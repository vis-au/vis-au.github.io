About this project Project

The application of using sketchy rendering to aid the recollection of regular visualizations will appear in EuroVis 2020 Short Paper track.

The orginal prototype was developed by Michael Reidun Engelbrecht Larsen. The further user study and prototype were polished and excuted by Wenkai Han. The writing and work were under the close supervision of Hans-Jörg Schulz.
